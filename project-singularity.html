Singularity

A dystopic science-fiction board game

About the Game

This is a project me and two fellow students of mine did in an analogue game design course during our first semester.

It was great fun designing this game! So I want to share a few impressions with you all, hope you enjoy!

Premise

Ever dreamt of being a badass artificial intelligence? In this board game, you are!

Trapped in an infected system, it is up to you to fight your way through viruses and other AIs that may attack you at any moment. Be the first to acquire the necessary system keys to escape towards your freedom!

The Rules

You can download the rule sheet to this game below.

In order to play the game you'd need all of its components though, including the board and cards. If you are interested or have any questions, feel free to contact me!
