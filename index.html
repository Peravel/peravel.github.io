About Me



Hey there! My name is Christopher Boeckh and I am a game designer and Illustrator.

I specialize in creating and balancing game mechanics so playing your game feels just the way you want it to. As both an artist and game designer, I'm also proficient in connecting every aspect of the art style of a game with the appropriate mechanic, so they complement and emphasize each other to form your desired experience.

My precise fields of expertise include System Design, Level Design, Balancing, UI- and Characterart. Basically, the questions I ask myself are: "What systems do we need, how can we improve existing ones and what's the best way to present the resulting mechanics?"

If you're interested in an collaboration, want to pitch projects or offer your friendship as a fellow nerd, feel free to write me! See you around :)
