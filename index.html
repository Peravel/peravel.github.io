CHRISTOPHER BOECKH


Game Designer




I grew up amidst the Black Forest, Germany. I’ve always been passionate about logical thinking, drawing, writing and generally visualizing my thoughts on paper. I love comfy bars, Japanese vending machines, spending time with my girlfriend and analyzing game mechanics.

I have studied Game Design at the University of Applied Sciences Europe in Berlin, Germany, where I found my passion for system design and communicating and realizing concepts.

What I do best is creating and adjusting game mechanics on paper and digitally and translating them into code. I particularily enjoy communicating how everything works to the team so everyone is on the same page and can work towards a common goal. For that purpose I create easy to read documents such as one page designs to make concepts understandable either for specific team members or the whole team.

If you like to collaborate with me, pitch projects or want to bond over our undying love to games, feel free to write me!
